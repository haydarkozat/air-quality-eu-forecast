## 🇬🇧 İngilizce versiyon

🌍 New project: **AI-Powered Air Quality Forecasting for European Cities**

Following my advanced STEM coursework, I built an end-to-end ML system aligned with the EU Green Deal and Zero Pollution Action Plan.

📊 **What it does:**
Predicts PM2.5 fine particulate matter concentrations 24 hours ahead for major European cities, with an interactive dashboard delivering health advisories.

🎯 **Why it matters:**
Air pollution causes 300,000+ premature deaths in the EU every year. Early-warning forecasts help sensitive groups (children, elderly, asthma patients) plan their day.

🔧 **Tech stack:**
• Python · Pandas · XGBoost · Streamlit · Plotly
• OpenAQ v3 API for real air quality data
• CodeCarbon to measure the model's own training emissions (Green AI principle)

✨ **Highlights:**
✓ Time-series-aware feature engineering (lags, rolling stats, cyclical encoding)
✓ Chronological train/test split — no data leakage
✓ Transparent carbon footprint reporting for the model itself
✓ Interactive Streamlit dashboard

📈 **Result:** ~3 μg/m³ MAE on 24-hour-ahead predictions — comparable to published research.
